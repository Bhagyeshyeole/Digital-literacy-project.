Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:

The Do’s

1) Google yourself. Purge the old cringe before a recruiter finds it.
2) Fix your LinkedIn. Clear wall photo + "CSE Student" headline = instant hire-ability.
3) The 10-minute rule. Type the angry reply, then delete it. Grab a chai instead.
4) Lurk with purpose. Follow tech leads on X or Discord to see how they actually talk.
5) Stay private. Keep the weekend chaos for friends and the public side professional.

The Don’ts

1) No live-posting. Wait until you leave the cafe before tagging the location.
2) Blur the barcodes. IDs and tickets are goldmines for data scammers.
3) Skip the beef. Arguing on Instagram won’t help you land an internship.
4) Stop reusing passwords. One leak shouldn't ruin your student portal or bank app.
5) Don’t be a code thief. Use open-source, but always give a shout-out to the creator.
